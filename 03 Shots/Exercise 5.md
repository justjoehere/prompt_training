## Key Takeaway

**The Pattern:**
- **Zero-Shot:** Fast but unpredictable
- **One-Shot:** Best balance for most business tasks
- **Few-Shot:** Consistent but slower

**The Decision:** Choose based on your priority - speed vs. reliability.
